Best Markets for Eredivisie Accas

Over 2.5 Goals

The Eredivisie averages 3.15 goals per game, the joint-highest of any major European league. Over 2.5 hits in 63% of all fixtures — making it an outstanding goals acca foundation.

BTTS

58% of Eredivisie fixtures see both teams score. Even games involving Ajax, PSV and Feyenoord at home often see opponents threaten their goal.

Win Accumulators

PSV and Ajax dominate domestically but the gap to mid-table is smaller than in the Bundesliga. Use them as anchor selections and build around them.

Total Goals Markets

3+ goals is achievable in over 55% of Eredivisie fixtures. Building a total goals acca using 3-6 Eredivisie matches can return big odds reliably.
