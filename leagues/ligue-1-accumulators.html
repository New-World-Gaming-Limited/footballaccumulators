Best Markets for Ligue 1 Accas

Win Accumulators

PSG dominate home fixtures with an exceptional win rate. Combine them with Monaco and Lille home selections for competitive Win acca odds.

Over 2.5 Goals

Ligue 1 averages 2.72 goals per game, and higher-profile fixtures regularly eclipse the 2.5 threshold. PSG vs any top-six rival is a reliable Over pick.

BTTS

With a 48% BTTS rate, selective use of BTTS in attacking fixtures is viable. Avoid defensive teams like Brest at home who regularly keep clean sheets.

Correct Score

PSG routinely score 3-4 goals at Parc des Princes. Correct score picks of 3-0 or 4-0 in one-sided home fixtures offer high-value acca legs.

Ligue 1 Accumulator FAQs

PSG win at home in over 80% of Ligue 1 fixtures, making them one of the most reliable Win acca selections in European football. However, their short odds (often 1.20-1.50) mean low acca contribution.

Ligue 1 reduced from 20 to 18 clubs from the 2023-24 season to improve competitive quality and allow clubs more recovery time between fixtures.

PSG home games, Monaco vs Lyon and Marseille vs Lyon are historically the most productive Over 2.5 fixtures. Defensive rivalries like Reims vs Brest are best avoided.

PSG's dominance keeps Win acca odds low. The best value typically comes from combining PSG home with Monaco home and a goals market in a longer accumulator.
