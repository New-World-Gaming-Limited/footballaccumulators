Best Markets for Premier League Accas

BTTS (Both Teams to Score)

With 52% BTTS rate, the Premier League is excellent for BTTS accumulators. Games involving mid-table teams with leaky defences are prime candidates.

Over 2.5 Goals

The high average goals per game (2.84) means Over 2.5 markets land well over half the time. Top 6 home fixtures are particularly reliable.

Win Accumulators

Home teams win 44% of the time. Backing strong favourites at home in single-sided fixtures offers solid acca foundations.

Goalscorer Markets

Reliable scorers like Haaland, Salah and Son make anytime scorer accumulators highly popular in the Premier League.

Key Teams to Watch

Manchester City ★★★★★

Dominant at home, average 2.8 goals scored per game. Excellent for Win and Over 2.5 selections.

Liverpool ★★★★☆

High-scoring but can ship goals. Strong BTTS profile, especially in European weeks.

Arsenal ★★★★☆

Tight defensively, strong at home. Good for Win selections but lower BTTS probability.

Newcastle United ★★★☆☆

Solid at St. James' Park, competitive BTTS profile away from home.

Aston Villa ★★★☆☆

Goals in both directions in European competition. High variance makes them interesting for BTTS accas.
