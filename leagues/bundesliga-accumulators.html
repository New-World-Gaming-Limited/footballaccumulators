Best Markets for Bundesliga Accas

Over 2.5 Goals

The Bundesliga averages over 3 goals per game — the highest of any top European league. Over 2.5 lands in over 61% of fixtures, making it the premier market for goal accumulators.

BTTS (Both Teams to Score)

With a 56% BTTS rate, the Bundesliga consistently delivers goals at both ends. Almost any fixture involving Bayern Munich away makes for a solid BTTS pick.

Win Accumulators

Bayern Munich's home dominance (80%+ win rate) provides a reliable anchor for Win accumulators. Combine with other home favourites for competitive odds.

Goalscorer Markets

High-scoring forwards like Harry Kane and Serhou Guirassy regularly hit double figures in goals — ideal for anytime scorer accumulator legs.
