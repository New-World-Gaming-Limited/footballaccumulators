The Scottish Premiership is Scotland's top football division, dominated by the Old Firm duo of Celtic and Rangers. Passionate crowds, intense rivalry matches and a huge gap between the top two and the rest makes it unique for acca bettors.

Best Markets for Scottish Premiership Accas

Win Accumulators

Celtic and Rangers win a combined 85%+ of league fixtures. Their very short odds make them best used as acca anchors to build on with other picks. Celtic v any bottom-6 side is a near-banker.

Asian Handicap

Given Celtic and Rangers' dominance, -2 or -3 Asian Handicap markets offer better value than straight Win. Against lower-half clubs they regularly score 4 or 5 goals.

BTTS in Old Firm Fixtures

The Old Firm Derby (Celtic vs Rangers) has seen BTTS Yes land in over 75% of recent editions. One of the most exciting BTTS acca selections in world football.

Over 2.5 Goals

With 53% of Scottish Premiership fixtures going Over 2.5, combining it with Old Firm matches and Edinburgh Derby (Hearts vs Hibs) adds variety to a goals acca.

Key Teams to Watch

Celtic ★★★★★

Dominant domestically for over a decade. Win rate in home fixtures exceeds 90% vs bottom-six teams. A near-banker Win acca selection at Celtic Park.

Rangers ★★★★☆

Strong home record at Ibrox. Win rate drops slightly against Hearts and Aberdeen away. Still one of the safest Win acca picks in Scottish football.

Hearts ★★★☆☆

Consistent top-four challengers, passionate Tynecastle atmosphere. Beat Rangers and Celtic on occasion — value selection for double-chance markets.

Aberdeen ★★☆☆☆

Challenging Celtic and Rangers more than in recent years. Good for Over 2.5 in matches against similarly-ranked opposition.

Hibernian ★★☆☆☆

Unpredictable, attack-minded. High BTTS profile in Edinburgh Derby. Worth including as a wildcard in BTTS accumulators.
