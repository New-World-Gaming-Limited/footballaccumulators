How to Use the Accumulator Calculator

Enter your total stake in the top field. Then type the decimal odds for each leg of your accumulator. The calculator updates your total odds, potential return and potential profit in real time. To add more legs, click the Add Leg button. To remove a leg, click the X next to it.

Understanding the Results

The Total Odds figure is the product of all your individual selection odds multiplied together. This is the combined decimal odds of your accumulator.

The Potential Return is your stake multiplied by the total odds. This is the full amount paid out if all selections win, including your original stake.

The Potential Profit is the return minus your stake - the actual profit if the acca lands.

Example

A 10 stake on a 4-fold with odds of 1.80, 2.00, 1.65 and 1.90 gives total odds of 1.80 x 2.00 x 1.65 x 1.90 = 11.25. Return = 10 x 11.25 = 112.50. Profit = 102.50.
