How to Use the Accumulator Builder

Click any odds button next to a match to add that selection to your accumulator slip. Only one pick per match is allowed - clicking a different outcome for the same match replaces your previous selection. Your combined odds and potential return update automatically.

Use the odds format buttons at the top to switch between decimal, fractional and American odds. The stake field defaults to 10 - change this to see the return for any stake amount.

Once you have built your acca, take your selections to your preferred bookmaker. We recommend comparing odds across multiple sites before placing - even small differences per leg compound significantly at accumulator level. Check our bookmakers page for the best acca promotions.

For a quick return calculation without the builder, use the standalone accumulator calculator. And if you are new to accumulator betting, our what is an accumulator guide is the best place to start.
