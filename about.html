About

Free football accumulator tips, tools and guides since 2020.

Our Mission

Football Accumulators was built by football fans who got tired of wading through generic betting sites to find genuinely useful information. We wanted a single destination for free acca tips, practical tools and honest guides - without the pushy ads and fake reviews.

Everything on this site is free. We do not charge for tips, calculators or guides. We may earn a commission if you sign up to a bookmaker through our links, but this does not influence our reviews or recommendations. We aim to recommend the bookmakers that genuinely offer the best experience for acca bettors, not the ones with the highest affiliate rates.

How We Pick Our Tips

Our tipsters analyse form, injuries, head-to-head records, league position and market movements before publishing selections each morning. We focus on seven accumulator markets: Win, BTTS, Over 2.5 Goals, Over 3.5 Goals, Correct Score, Goalscorer and weekly specials.

We do not guarantee profits. Football betting is inherently unpredictable and even the best-researched tips lose regularly. The goal is to provide well-reasoned selections that have genuine backing - not just randomly chosen games to fill a page.

Our Tools

The accumulator builder lets you build your own acca from today's matches with live combined odds. The acca calculator handles any number of legs and shows return and profit instantly. Our calculator suite includes eight tools covering odds conversion, margin, dutching, each-way, lay betting, bet returns and arbitrage.

Responsible Gambling

We take responsible gambling seriously. Football betting should be entertaining, not a source of financial or emotional harm. We encourage all visitors to:

  • Set a fixed budget for betting and never exceed it
  • Never chase losses
  • Use the free tools available at BeGambleAware.org
  • Use bookmaker responsible gambling tools such as deposit limits and self-exclusion
  • Take breaks from betting if it stops being enjoyable

If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, help is available. Visit GamCare or call the National Gambling Helpline on 0808 8020 133 (free, 24/7).

Contact

We are a small team and read every message we receive. If you have a suggestion, a tip to share, or a question about any of our tools, feel free to get in touch via our social channels or by email. Links are in the footer.
